Pasalubong is a Filipino tradition deeply rooted in the country’s culture of hospitality and thoughtfulness. The term comes from the Filipino word "salubong," meaning "to meet" or "to welcome," and it refers to gifts or souvenirs brought by travelers for their loved ones after a trip.
